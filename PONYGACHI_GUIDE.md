# Ponygachi Bot - Complete Guide

## Overview
Ponygachi (@Ponygatchi_bot) is a Tamagotchi-style game where you raise and race your pixel pony to earn real $PONY tokens!

## Goal
Level up your pony and earn $PONY rewards through racing and training!

## Energy System
- **Energy is required** for racing and training
- **Feed your pony** with Grain to restore energy
- **Max energy:** 100
- Energy depletes when you train or race

## Grain Economy
Grain is the in-game currency used to feed your pony.

### How to Earn Grain:
- **Daily check-in:** +20 Grain
- **Tweet about the game:** +20 Grain
- **Chat in the group:** Earn grain for being active
- Complete other tasks as they become available

### How to Use Grain:
- **Feed:** -10 Grain ‚Üí +50 Energy

## Actions

### Feed
- **Cost:** 10 Grain
- **Effect:** +50 Energy
- **Use when:** Your pony's energy is low

### Train
- **Cost:** 50 Energy
- **Effect:** +50 XP
- **Use when:** You want to level up but don't want to race

### Race
- **Cost:** 75 Energy
- **Effect:** +100 XP + $PONY rewards (if you place)
- **Best action:** Highest XP gain and chance to earn $PONY!

## Earning $PONY

### Race Rewards:
- **1st place:** 5M $PONY + 100 bonus XP
- **2nd place:** 3M $PONY
- **3rd place:** 1.5M $PONY
- **Higher levels = higher multiplier!**

The more you level up, the more $PONY you earn per race!

## Strategy Tips

### Optimal Gameplay Loop:
1. **Check in daily** for free grain
2. **Tweet about the game** for more grain
3. **Feed your pony** when energy is low
4. **Race frequently** for XP and $PONY
5. **Level up** to increase earning multiplier
6. **Claim rewards** on your preferred chain

### Energy Management:
- Keep energy above 75 to race
- Feed strategically - don't waste grain when energy is high
- Race > Train (racing gives more XP and $PONY)

### Grain Management:
- Do daily tasks consistently
- Tweet regularly for easy grain
- Chat in the group for bonus grain
- Save grain for when you need to race

5. **Level up:** Increase your earning potential
6. **Claim rewards:** Withdraw $PONY to your wallet

## Key Differences from Pixel Pony Racing

### Ponygachi:
- Tamagotchi-style care game
- Earn $PONY by keeping pony alive and racing
- Level-based progression
- Daily engagement rewards
- Free to play (no betting required)

### Pixel Pony Racing:
- Instant 16-horse betting game
- Bet $PONY to win up to 10x
- No leveling system
- Pay entry fee per race
- Gambling/betting focused

Both games use $PONY tokens but serve different purposes!
